Biography

I'm a Post-doctoral fellow at the School of Computer Science and Technology, Beijing Institute of Technology, advised by Prof. Ronghua Li. Previously, I received my PhD degree from Beijing Institute of Technology in 2022, advised by Prof. Guoren Wang.

My general research interest lies in graph data management, particularly cohesive subgraph search and graph algorithms.
